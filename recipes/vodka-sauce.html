Best Ever Spirit Free Vodka Sauce

Description

I'm going to let you guys in on a little secret, vodka sauce does NOT need vodka to be good! In fact, this one is 100x better in my opinion. This is a nearly classic and absolutely delicious rendition of the famous vodka sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 45g unsalted butter
  • 1 roughly chopped red onion
  • 3 cloves of garlic, smashed
  • 1 tbsp red pepper flakes
  • 130g tomato paste
  • 400g crushed tomatoes
  • 240g heavy cream
  • 55g finely grated parmesan cheese
  • 227g penne pasta

Method of Preparation

  • 1. First add about 2tbsp of oil to a sauce pot or dutch oven. Heat slowly on med until oil is shimmering.
  • 2. Once oil is preheated, add butter, onion, garlic and red pepper flakes and sweat until the onions are translucent.
  • 3. Add your tomatoes and tomato paste. Reduce heat to med-low and cook for 30 minutes uncovered.
  • 4. While this is cooking, fill another pot with water, liberally season with salt and bring to a boil.
  • 5. After 30 minutes, add sauce to a blender with the cream and parmesan. Blend until very smooth. Optionally you can pass it through a fine mesh strainer after to remove any bits that didn't blend properly.
  • 6. Cook your pasta according to the directions on the box or to your desired level of doneness.
  • 7. Strain the pasta, add back to the pot along with your sauce. Mix to combine and serve!
  • 8. If you want to spice up your presentation a little, grate some fresh parmesan on top and throw a couple fresh basil leaves on there!
