Learn some fun new professional kitchen terms to impress at your next dinner party!

Bouquet Garni

A collection of aromatics used to flavor stocks, soups, and stews.

Fabricate

Breaking down a protein from its whole form into servable portions.

Glace

A white or brown stock reduced by 85%-90% through simmering to intensify its flavor.

Singer

A process in which the pan that is used to saute an entree or the food itself is dusted with flour. The fat then absorbs the flour, forming a roux.

Socles

The food items used to raise the height of the main items to make them more prominent on the plate; must be edible and compliment the dish as a whole.

Supreme

The segment of a citrus fruit that is cut away from the membrane on the inside to give it a more delicate texture and appealing appearance.
